Aesthetic Atoms

This is a demonstration of an application of my p5.js component. Use the controls below to change the appearance of the sketch on the right. Use the mouse to drag around the atom and scroll to zoom in and out.

See the documentation (README.md) for details on what each attribute does.






Notes:

  • Noise decreases performance slightly.
  • Smoothing increases visual refinement at the cost of a decreased framerate.
  • Draw to Cube: Drawing a 3D sketch to a 3D object results in unexpected graphical glitches and/or behaviour. This functionality is included purely as a proof of concept.
